Summer Cooking Recipes

1. Summer Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of lettuce
  • 1 sliced cucumber
  • 1 sliced tomato
  • Your choice of dressing

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Toss with your choice of dressing.
  3. Serve chilled.

2. Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium high heat.
  2. Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill for 6-7 minutes on each side or until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Serve with summer salad.
